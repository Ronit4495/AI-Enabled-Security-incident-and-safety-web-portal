File a Cyber Crime Complaint Online

Use this portal to report cyber crimes committed against you or someone you know. All complaints are handled confidentially and forwarded to the appropriate State/UT law enforcement agencies.

  • Report crimes related to women and children anonymously
  • Financial fraud complaints processed within 24 hours
  • Automatic case ID generated for tracking
  • Reports forwarded to respective state police
  • Zero cost — no fee charged to file a complaint
  • Evidence preservation guidance available

Stay Safe Online

Cyber Safety Tips

🔐

Protect Your OTP

Never share OTP, PIN, or password with anyone — not even bank officials. Banks never ask for these details over calls.

🔗

Verify Before You Click

Check URLs carefully before entering credentials. Fraudsters create near-identical fake websites to steal your data.

📱

Secure Your Devices

Keep software updated, use strong passwords, enable 2FA, and never install apps from unknown sources.

💸

Think Before You Transfer

Pause before sending money online. If something feels rushed or too good to be true, it likely is a scam.
